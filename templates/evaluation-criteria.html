Evaluation Criteria


# Criteria Description
1 Job Knowledge Knows their job and fulfills their JD to the fullest potential.
2 Quality of Work Is thorough, accurate, and neat in work.
3 Accountability Accepts responsibility for actions, answerable to consequences.
4 Attitude/Respectfulness Shows initiative, optimism, and politeness.
5 Punctuality & Attendance Is rarely absent, arrives punctually, works required hours.
6 Policy & Procedure Follows the organization's policies and procedures.
7 Compellation of Assignment Successfully completes tasks and meets all deadlines.
8 Confidentiality Does not discuss internal events with coworkers.
9 Accepts criticism Has the ability to learn from suggestions and change behavior.
10 Appearance of Work Area Keeps work area neat and orderly.
